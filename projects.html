Visualization Analysis of the Development of Theme and Content in Suspenseful Detective Novels in the Past 20 Years.

Jun 17 2022
This project analyzed the trends in popularity of suspenseful detective novels from 1940 to 2022, and found an increasing preference among Chinese readers for Japanese works. By comparing the complexity of character relationships and the use of keywords in different works, changes in the types of works and audience preferences were observed. Meanwhile, search frequency for related keywords indicated a decline in the popularity of "suspenseful novels", with readers turning to other areas.

Jul 9 2021

This project aims to use an autoencoder for unsupervised anomaly detection, where the positive class is set as images of airplanes and the negative class is composed of images of cars, birds, cats, deer, dogs, frogs, horses, ships, and trucks. The anomaly samples are not included in the learning process.
