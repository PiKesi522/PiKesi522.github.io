V4VRIN: Visual Analysis of Value-guided Regional Industry Network Constructed by Graph Structure Learning

Apr 1 2023
In this paper, we propose a value-guided regional industry network (VRIN) for the first time to describe the potential value-guided economic linkages between regional industries. An unsupervised directed graph structure learning framework is also proposed oriented to the extraction of hidden associations.
