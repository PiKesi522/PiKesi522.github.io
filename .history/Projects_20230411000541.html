Visualization Analysis of the Development of Theme and Content in Suspenseful Detective Novels in the Past 20 Years.

Jun 17 2022
This project aims to show the trends in popularity of suspenseful detective novels from 1940 to 2022, and to explore any potential connections and commonalities.
Before the millennium, European and American suspenseful detective novels were more popular among Chinese readers, but now Japanese works are more popular.
The study compared the complexity of character relationships in traditional European and American works to that in modern Japanese works, and analyzed the use of keywords in the latter. It also examined search frequencies for related terms on search engines, and found that the popularity of "suspenseful novels" has been declining in recent years.

Research on Convex Optimization Methods for Solving Lasso Problems

Jul 26 2022
This paper focuses on studying convex optimization methods for solving Lasso problems, including gradient descent, coordinate descent, and ADMM algorithms. The study compares and analyzes the performance of different algorithms on various datasets and discusses their pros, cons, and practical applications in machine learning, particularly in feature selection.
